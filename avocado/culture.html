afm culture

Our organization doesn’t rely on a vision or a mission statement. Instead, we believe in our culture as an organic guiding force that will naturally and organically align individuals and teams. That culture is encapsulated in our company manifesto, and in our 10 operating guidelines, which are even written in our office’s walls to encourage, empower and inspire all individuals to live by them.

OPERATING GUIDELINES

Believe in the Power of Brand Building

We are passionate brand builders, culinary experts, extraordinary story tellers, marketing disrupters, and overachievers with a creative mind, bold goals, and a restless drive.

Spread the Vibrant Magic of Mexicanity

Act as a Responsible Category Leader

We’ve placed value over price in our consumers mind.

Educate Educated Educate

Celebrate Wins and then Improve Them

Develop the Best People in the Best Industry

Ultimately brands are the people who build them and ours is as unique as each of us.

Work Hard and Always Have Fun

Always Focus On Value, Not Price

Our motivation is driven by making a difference in our industry, for our consumers, and for the thousands of growers, packers, and importers how trust us with the fruit of their labor.

Be An Outlier and Break the Produce Mold

Be the Strategic Category Catalyst

WHAT IS MEXICANITY?

Mexicanity is a celebration of vibrant and meaningful moments in your everyday life through a connection with Mexican culture. It is fun. It is colorful. It's about you and your friends and family sharing a magical moment around a delicious bowl of guac.

THIS IS US...

We are proud to represent 19,000 producers, more than 40 packaging corporations, and small family farms run by second and third-generation avocado growers, 80 percent of whom own less than five hectors of land.

Core Competencies

Our team works cooperatively and flexibly with others with the full understanding of their role to be played as team member through collaboration with coworkers, encouraging synergy and inclusion, removing barriers, and building positively and respect.

Culture COMMITTEE

We have a Cultural Committee formed by a group of non-executive employees. The company assigns an annual budget to this initiative, which is allocated by the Committee toward the organization of events and activities aimed to allow the exercise of the operation guidelines which strengthens the culture.

These events and initiatives are conceived, planned and executed by the junior employees, which gives them the opportunity to distill their own creative ideas into the culture of the organization which gets shaped on an ongoing basis.

lets Be MÁs

Let's Be Más is our mindset of greatness, being diverse, and going the extra mile while staying true to ourselves and celebrating our team’s vibrant culture.

AFM LIFE

At Avocados from Mexico, we believe that our employees are the heart of the company and our most essential asset. It all starts with the richness of our AFM Life that empowers our AFM Family with the resources, incentives, flexibility, and programs that will support the enrichment on the job, and the well-being of family Life. Some of those rewards come in different forms such as the following:

Protecting your Family as you can’t predict the future, but you can prepare for it through our medical and income protection plans;

Preparing you for the Future with our financial retirement plan;

Your Professional Development that expands your power skills and personal and functional learnings;

Our Work-Life Integration that offers leave, paid time off, health and wellness, and telework days;

A Working Environment that helps you be at our best with the freedom to be creative; &

An AFM Life value system of heart and care that focuses on preserving our AFM Family.
